Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 19, 2026

At SaigoBits (accessible from SaigoBits.com), we prioritize the privacy of our visitors and clients. This Privacy Policy details the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the safeguards we take to keep your information secure.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect personal information that you voluntarily choose to provide to us. This happens when you fill out our contact form hosted on Google Forms. The information collected may include:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address
  • Company or business name
  • Project descriptions, details, and requirements

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in the following ways:

  • To understand your project goals and requirements.
  • To draft proposal documents, estimates, and project scopes.
  • To communicate with you regarding your inquiry or contract updates.
  • To maintain records of past collaborations and client relations.
  • We do not sell, rent, or distribute your contact details to third-party advertisers.

3. Data Protection & Processing

Our contact form is hosted via Google Forms. By completing the form, you acknowledge that the information you provide will be transferred to Google for processing in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. We store the responses securely in our private workspace and take all reasonable technical and operational measures to prevent unauthorized data access.

4. Intellectual Property & Confidentiality

As a software development and IT consultancy, we respect your proprietary business concepts. Any business ideas, blueprints, databases, or trade secrets shared with us during pre-contract discussions are treated as strictly confidential. Upon establishing a contract, we routinely enter into mutual Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs).

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to request access to the information we hold about you, request corrections to any inaccuracies, or ask that we completely erase your contact data from our system. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please submit a request via the contact form on our home page.
