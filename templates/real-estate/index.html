Frequently Asked Questions

Need clarifications regarding property registrations, stamp duty rates, site visits, or home loan applications? Read our detailed responses.

Every listed project is subjected to rigorous legal checks. We verify building plans, land title ownership histories (up to 30 years), RERA approvals, municipal non-encumbrance declarations, and builder financial health status prior to onboarding them.

Under Reserve Bank regulations in India, lenders require a minimum of 10% to 20% down payment depending on property valuations. Our calculator automatically computes this 10% lower limit of property values, letting you evaluate ranges up to 80% to plan loans.

Registration rates are set by state governments. Generally, stamp duty fees range from 4% to 8% of purchase values, plus a 1% court registration fee. These costs are external to base property pricing and must be factored into loan calculations.
